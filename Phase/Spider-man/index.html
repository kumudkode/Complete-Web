The Web's Skeleton

Spider-man is a superhero created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962.

Origin Story

Peter Parker, a high school student, gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his powers to fight crime and protect New York City.

Key Events

  • Bitten by a radioactive spider
  • Uncle Ben's death
  • First battle with the Green Goblin

These events shape Peter's journey as Spider-man.

Abilities

  1. Superhuman strength
  2. Wall-crawling
  3. Spider-sense
  4. Web-shooting

Villains

  • Green Goblin
  • Doctor Octopus
  • Venom
  • Sandman
